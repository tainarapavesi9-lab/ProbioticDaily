By utilizing advanced delayed-release capsule technology, Bioma ensures that billions of active, beneficial bacteria survive stomach acid to reach your gut alive. This promotes stable energy throughout the day and helps keep your digestive system running like clockwork.

For best results, take Bioma consistently once a day with a glass of water. While Bioma does the heavy lifting, staying hydrated and eating a balanced, fiber-rich diet will help the beneficial bacteria thrive even faster.
